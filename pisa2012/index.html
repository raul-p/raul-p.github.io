PISA 2012: What makes good students?

Is there a connection between PISA scores and other variables?

PISA is a survey of students' skills and knowledge as they approach the end of compulsory education.
It assessed the competencies of 15-year-olds in reading, mathematics and science in 65 countries and economies.

This interactive graph allow you to explore the relations between mean students' scores per country and some variables.
